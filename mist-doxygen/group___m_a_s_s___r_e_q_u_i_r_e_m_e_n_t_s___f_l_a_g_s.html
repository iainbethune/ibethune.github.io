Detailed Description

combinations of these flags may be returned by GetMassRequirements()

MIST Integrators make use of particle masses, or derived properties which can be precomputed once at the beginning of the simulation, when the MD code calls MIST_SetMassPtr(). Depending on the flags set in the integrator's constructor, the System::SetMassPtr() function will do any computation that is required.
